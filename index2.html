Why Nu?

Designed for the modern developer who values efficiency without sacrificing safety.

High Density Packing

Like the scales of a pangolin, syntax is tightly packed. Compress `struct` to `S` and `return` to `<`. Optimized for developer velocity.

Armored Safety

Inherits 100% of Rust's memory safety guarantees. The "scales" don't just look cool; they protect your memory model.

AI-Native Systems

Built for the AI era with optimized context usage. First-class tooling for LLM-assisted coding workflows.
